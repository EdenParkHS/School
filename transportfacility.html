Bus Registration Charges of $ 20/- shall have to be paid in addition to bus charges at the beginning of each academic year. Fees will be charged for all 12 months. A parent, who withdraws the transport facility before the vacations and rejoins after the vacations, may not be given the transport facility. The amount is payable along with the school fees on a quarterly basis.
