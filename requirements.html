Requirements

Nursery Admission 2017

Age Criteria: A child who has completed 3 years as on March 31 of the year of admission is eligible for admission.


Issue and download of application form: Registration form for Nursery Admission 2015 can be downloaded from School’s website or can be collected from school’s office free of cost.


Registration:Duly filled and attested (signed) application form along with required documents should be submitted at school office along with registration fee of $ 10 before the last date of form submission.


Documents Required for Admission:-:

  • •Attested photocopy of birth certificate

  • •Attested photocopy of SC/ST category certificate

  • •One passport sized photographer of the child, child’s father and mother.


Seat Reservation and criteria for admission is as per the guidelines followed by the institute.


Class XI Admission 2017

Class XI Admission in schools for the academic year 2017 would made on the basis of grade/percentage of marks obtained in Class X under state board. Candidates wishing to apply for admission are required to do so online. In case of non availability of seats, Schools conduct an admission test for class XI students based on class X syllabus. This is a multiple objective type test with questions in all subjects expect English.


Eligibility: Grade or percentage of marks obtained Class X under state board.


Documents required for Admission:- •Attested photocopy of municipal birth certificate. (Original to be produced at the time of admission).
•Two passport sized photographs.
•School Leaving Certificate duly countersigned.
•Medical fitness certificate from a competent doctor.
•Character Certificate from last attended school.


Respective school managements reserve the right to add, change or amend any of the rules and regulations pertaining to admission.

This would be binding on all students and parents.
